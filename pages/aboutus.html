About Us

Goolden Bangla Ideal School is a Jewish International School committed to academic excellence through fostering intellectual curiosity, self-directed learning and the individual development of each child.



Our History

Goolden Bangla Ideal School was established in 2021 and in the nearly three decades since its founding, Goolden Bangla Ideal School has earned recognition as a superb educational institution—rigorous, traditional and innovative—an institution dedicated to intellectual achievement, and reflecting a tight-knit, welcoming community, supportive of students and profoundly committed to their growth and well-being.
